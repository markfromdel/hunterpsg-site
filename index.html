Holistic Performance Approach

Addressing performance desires using established frameworks.

1

Diagnose

Use HPT models (Gilbert, Rummler-Brache) to separate skill issues from environmental or process barriers. We validate the problem before prescribing training.

2

Design

Apply FSU-style ISD and systems engineering together: performance objectives → instructional strategy → job aids → integration with actual systems.

3

Deliver & Sustain

Implement, observe, and measure. Support acceptance testing, operator onboarding, and drills. Keep it lightweight and repeatable for the agency.
